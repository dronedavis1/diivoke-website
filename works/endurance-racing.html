Project Overview

Diivoke covers the world of endurance racing for HAX and Ironman 70.3 — triathlon race weekends, training buildup, and sponsor content that captures the grind behind the finish line.

Each video is built to hype a race weekend, honor participants and their training, or promote an event sponsor or partner brand.

On Site

We're out on course from packet pickup through the finish chute, capturing pre-race energy, athlete moments, and the crowd along the route.

Fast turnaround keeps the content relevant while race weekend excitement is still high.

Final Outcome

The finished videos are used across event and sponsor social channels to celebrate finishers and build hype for the next race on the calendar.
