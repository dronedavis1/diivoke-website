Jacksonville GFL Class I Transfer Station

Project Overview

GFL Environmental contracted Green Rock Strategies who hired Diivoke to document the transformation of an existing Jacksonville warehouse into a fully operational Class I Transfer Station. The goal was to capture the scale, scope, and craftsmanship of the build for use in corporate and promotional materials.

We focused on showcasing the facility's infrastructure, machinery, and operational flow — giving viewers a clear picture of what goes into a project of this size.

On Site

We arrived on location and documented the facility from multiple angles — wide establishing shots of the building exterior, interior infrastructure, active equipment, and the overall scale of the operation.

Drone footage was used to capture the full footprint of the facility and surrounding property, giving the final edit a cinematic quality that static shots alone couldn't achieve.

Final Outcome

The delivered package included a polished highlight reel and raw b-roll footage ready for use across GRS's and GFL's corporate communications, social media, and internal presentations.

The footage gave GFL a professional, visual record of the Jacksonville facility that accurately represents the scale and capability of their operations.

Project Overview

GFL Environmental brought in Green Rock Strategies, who partnered with Diivoke, to document their recycling facility in Lakeland, FL. The goal was to show how the site sorts, processes, and moves recyclable material at scale for use in operational and marketing materials.

We focused on the sorting lines, material handling equipment, and the rhythm of a working recycling floor — capturing the process from intake to output.

On Site

We spent the day inside the facility filming the sorting and processing lines in motion, along with wide shots of the loading and staging areas outside.

Drone footage captured the full scale of the Lakeland property, giving the final video a broader sense of the facility's size and layout.

Final Outcome

The finished video gives GFL and Green Rock Strategies a clear, professional look at how the Lakeland facility operates day to day.

It's now used across corporate communications and internal training materials to showcase the site's recycling process.
