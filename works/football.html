Project Overview

Diivoke provides ongoing video coverage for flag and tackle football programs in the Gainesville area, capturing the energy of game day for teams, families, and league promotion.

Each video is built to hype up a specific matchup, season milestone, or team moment.

On Site

We're on the sideline and in the stands for kickoff, capturing live action, crowd energy, and standout plays as they happen.

Footage is turned around quickly so teams can share the hype while the moment is still fresh.

Final Outcome

The finished recaps and hype videos are shared across team and league social channels, driving engagement and building excitement for the next game.
