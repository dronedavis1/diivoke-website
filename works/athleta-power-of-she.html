Athleta | The Power of She

A self-directed mock commercial for Athleta celebrating female athletes and the power of movement.

3 Days COMMERCIAL

Project Overview

This self-directed mock commercial was built to showcase Diivoke's ability to conceive, shoot, and edit high-energy athletic content in the style of a national Athleta campaign.

We focused on movement, silhouette, and light — creating a visual language that celebrates strength and momentum.

On Set

Shot against natural light with a focus on motion blur and dynamic framing, the sequence follows an athlete through a series of movement studies.

Multiple takes were captured at varying shutter speeds to give the editor flexibility in post to dial in the exact energy of each cut.

Final Outcome

The final edit delivered a polished 30-second spot demonstrating Diivoke's commercial production capabilities for athletic and lifestyle brands.

This project now serves as a core reel piece for pitching sports and apparel clients.
