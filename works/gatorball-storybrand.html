Gatorball Baseball Academy Storybrand

A storybrand video for Gatorball Baseball Academy in Gainesville, FL built to connect with families and recruit the next generation of athletes.

1 Day SPORTS

Project Overview

Gatorball Baseball Academy needed a StoryBrand-style video to help families understand their coaching philosophy and connect emotionally with the program.

We built the narrative around real coaches, real players, and the academy's actual training environment in Gainesville, FL.

On Location

We spent the day filming live practice sessions, one-on-one coaching moments, and interviews with coaches and parents.

Natural, candid footage was prioritized over staged shots to keep the story authentic to the academy's culture.

Final Outcome

The finished video is used as the centerpiece of Gatorball's enrollment page, helping convert interested families into signed-up athletes.

The academy reported a noticeable increase in inquiry-to-enrollment conversion after launch.
