Project Overview

Diivoke partners with individual real estate agents across Florida, USA to produce property walkthroughs, aerial drone footage, and social-ready video content that helps listings stand out.

Each agent gets a tailored visual package built around the specific property and its surrounding area.

On Site

For every property we capture wide establishing shots, interior walkthroughs, and drone footage of the surrounding neighborhood — giving agents a complete visual package for each listing.

Drone photography is also used on its own for scouting and lifestyle content, like the Atlantic Beach aerial series.

Final Outcome

The finished videos and photos are used across MLS listings, social media, and agent marketing materials, helping properties sell faster and giving agents a professional edge in a competitive market.
